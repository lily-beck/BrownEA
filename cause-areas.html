Cause Areas

The problems we focus on

We explore cause areas based on their scale, tractability, and neglectedness — not just their visibility.

Global Health & Development
Preventing easily treatable diseases, expanding access to effective interventions, and reducing extreme poverty.
🤖
AI Safety
Ensuring transformative AI systems are developed safely and in ways that benefit humanity as a whole.
🐾
Animal Welfare
Reducing the suffering of animals in factory farming and improving conditions for billions of sentient beings.
⏳
Existential Risk
Protecting the long-term future by reducing risks from pandemics, nuclear weapons, and other global catastrophes.
