Programs

The Intro Fellowship

Our flagship 8-week program for students who want to explore the ideas and practice of effective altruism.

01
Learn the fundamentals
Each week covers a core idea in EA — from the basics of impartial altruism to cause prioritization and career planning.
02
Weekly discussion & readings
We meet weekly for guided discussion over snacks. No prior knowledge required — just curiosity and a willingness to think carefully.
03
Career workshops
Explore how to apply EA ideas to your own career — whether in research, policy, tech, medicine, or beyond.
04
Form your own views
The fellowship is not about converting you. It's about helping you reason carefully about how to make a difference.
05
Join the community
Fellows become part of an ongoing community of EA-curious students at Brown and beyond.
