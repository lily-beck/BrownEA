About Us

A community built around
rigorous, compassionate action

Brown's Effective Altruism Club brings together students who want to use their time and resources to have the greatest possible positive impact on the world. We're not just a discussion group — we're a community actively engaged in some of the most important questions of our time.

We host speaker events, reading groups, and discussions focused on high-impact careers, global health, existential risks, and other core topics in effective altruism. Whether you're brand new or deeply familiar with EA, there's a place for you here.

Effective altruism isn't a fixed dogma. It's a commitment to asking hard questions, following the evidence, and acting on what we find — even when the answers are surprising.

  • Evidence over intuition
    We use data and careful reasoning to identify where our efforts go furthest — not just what feels good.
  • Open inquiry
    We hold our views with humility and update them when we encounter better arguments. Debate is welcome.
  • Real-world action
    We connect ideas to careers, donations, and community — translating values into tangible impact.
  • Inclusive community
    Anyone curious about doing the most good is welcome, regardless of background, major, or experience level.
